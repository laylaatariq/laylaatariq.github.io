Cloudiness

The percentage of cloudiness is scattered all over the palce. Most of the cities share the same percentage of cloudiness, only a few of them differ that too when we reach a percentage above 90. However, there are more cities to the east of the equator that experience cloudiness than to those in the south. Intresting thing to notice here is that most of the cities share the same percentage of cloudiness below 90%, but as we touch the 90% mark, the straight line distorts.
